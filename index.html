Information

 

Hours of Operation

 

Wiggles and Giggles Home Daycare is open 24 hours, 7 days a week. There are a few options for care. We offer full time care any 5 days of the week with a max of 10 hours per day. Part-time services are offered for any 3 days of the week with a max of 10 hours per day. We also offer daily rates at full day, any day of the week with a max of only 10 hours.

 

Enrollment

 

Before your child can be officially enrolled in Wiggles and Giggles Home Daycare you must complete and provide the following documents:

• Signed Parent Contract and Rate Agreement
• Completed Child Profile and Copy of Immunization Record
• Signed Consent Forms (those that are applicable)
• Registration fee must be paid ($50)
 

A non-refundable registration fee of $50.00 is charged upon registration of each child. Spaces will not be held by verbal contract, registration fee must be paid in order for a spot to be held for your child.

About The Owner

Sandi Lynch

 

Being a mother to three beautiful children, I knew that I wanted to dedicate my life to caring and teaching children. This is why I decided to open the doors to my home daycare. Working with children is a blessing to me as I get to be apart of their growth and development. Together we create a very fun energetic atmosphere that is packed with learning opportunities. Children inspire me to be a better me, because shaping young minds and showing love and compassion, is what I have always loved to do. It is our duty to protect, nurture, and love our children. This is how we shape the leaders of our future.
